Why Trisquel?

Trisquel is entirely free software and encourages users to value and protect their freedom. Other distributions encourage the use of propietary software while Trisquel has been designed to offer you and recommend only free software.

The project aims for a fully free software system without proprietary software or firmware and uses a version of Ubuntu's modified kernel, with the non-free code (binary blobs) removed.

Learn more about free software

The Free OS: Libre and Gratis

Like most of the GNU/Linux distributions, Trisquel is a just a download away, free of charge.

The project is mainly funded by donations so, if you enjoy Trisquel, we hope you will support it either with a one time donation or become a Trisquel Associate Member and support the project with a recurring donation.

Experience the full power of Linux

Trisquel brings you an operating system that is tight, beautiful, and robust. We want your software to be feature-rich and work exactly as you expect it to and we will never compromise your freedom.

Trisquel comes packaged with the applications that you will most likely need for your daily use.

We respect your privacy

At the Trisquel GNU/Linux project we value and strive to respect your privacy. Your data always belongs to you, and only you. We don’t make advertising deals or collect sensitive personal data. We’re funded directly by our users paying what they want for Trisquel.

We do not collect any data from Trisquel. Your files, settings, and all other personal data remain on-device unless you explicitly share them with a third-party app or service.
